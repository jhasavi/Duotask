## App Store Description

### Short Description (30 words)
**DuoTask** - The simple, beautiful way for couples and partners to share and manage tasks together. Perfect for household chores, errands, and daily responsibilities.

### Full Description

**Transform your relationship through shared task management!**

DuoTask is the beautiful, simple task manager designed specifically for couples and partners who want to work together seamlessly. Whether you're managing household chores, planning errands, or coordinating daily responsibilities, DuoTask makes teamwork effortless and enjoyable.

**🎯 Perfect for Couples & Partners**
• Share tasks with your significant other, roommate, or partner
• Real-time synchronization so you're always on the same page
• Beautiful visual interface that makes task management enjoyable
• Voice-friendly design for use while walking or driving

**✨ Beautiful & Intuitive**
• Color-coded task bubbles for easy visual recognition
• Single-tap status changes (Unclaimed → Claimed → Done)
• Long-press for quick deletion
• Smooth animations and delightful micro-interactions

**🔄 Smart Task Management**
• Create repeatable tasks for daily, weekly, or monthly chores
• Set due dates and get gentle reminders
• Mark urgent tasks that need immediate attention
• Auto-delete completed tasks to keep your list clean

**👥 Multi-Partner Ready**
• Switch between different partners (spouse, roommate, friend)
• Keep track of frequent partners for quick access
• One-time pairing codes for easy connection
• Partner history and statistics

**🚶‍♂️ Designed for Real Life**
• Large touch targets for easy one-handed use
• Walking/driving mode for on-the-go task management
• Voice-friendly interface with haptic feedback
• Works perfectly while you're busy with other activities

**💕 Relationship Enhancement**
• Celebrate achievements together with beautiful animations
• Track progress and see your teamwork in action
• Gentle reminders that support rather than nag
• Positive reinforcement for completed tasks

**🔒 Privacy & Security**
• End-to-end encryption for your data
• Secure authentication with Supabase
• Your tasks stay private between you and your partner
• No data mining or intrusive tracking

**Perfect for:**
• Couples managing household responsibilities
• Roommates coordinating shared chores
• Partners planning errands and tasks
• Anyone who wants to work together more effectively

**Why DuoTask?**
Unlike complex project management tools, DuoTask is designed specifically for the simple, everyday tasks that couples and partners share. It's beautiful, intuitive, and actually enjoyable to use - making teamwork feel less like work and more like a shared adventure.

Download DuoTask today and discover how simple, beautiful task sharing can strengthen your relationship and make your daily life more organized and harmonious!
