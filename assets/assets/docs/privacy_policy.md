# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: October 31, 2025**

## Introduction

Welcome to DuoTask ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our task-sharing application.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide

**Account Information:**
- Email address
- Display name
- Password (encrypted)
- Profile picture (optional)

**Task Data:**
- Task titles and descriptions
- Due dates and priorities
- Task status and assignments
- Recurrence settings

**Usage Data:**
- Login timestamps
- Feature usage patterns
- App interactions
- Device information

### 1.2 Automatically Collected Information

**Technical Data:**
- IP address
- Device type and model
- Operating system
- Browser type and version
- App version

**Analytics Data:**
- Pages/screens viewed
- Time spent in app
- Feature usage statistics
- Error logs and crash reports

### 1.3 Third-Party Data

**Google OAuth:**
- Google account ID
- Email address
- Profile name
- Profile picture (if authorized)

**Push Notifications:**
- Device tokens (for notifications)
- Notification preferences

### 2.2 Improve Services
- Analyze usage patterns
- Fix bugs and errors
- Develop new features
- Optimize performance
- Conduct research

### 2.3 Communicate
- Send service updates
- Notify about account changes
- Respond to inquiries
- Share important announcements

### 2.4 Security
- Detect and prevent fraud

- Enforce our terms of service

## 4. Data Security

### 4.1 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures:

**Encryption:**
- Data encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- Passwords hashed using bcrypt
- Sensitive data encrypted at rest

**Access Controls:**
- Row-level security policies
- Role-based access control
- Multi-factor authentication support

**Monitoring:**
- Regular security audits
- Intrusion detection systems
- Error monitoring and alerts

### 4.2 Your Responsibility

You are responsible for:
- Keeping your password secure
- Not sharing your account
- Logging out on shared devices
- Reporting suspicious activity

### 4.3 Limitations

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 5. Data Retention

### 5.1 Active Accounts
- We retain your data as long as your account is active
- Tasks and history are preserved for your convenience

### 5.2 Deleted Accounts
- Account data is permanently deleted within 30 days
- Backup copies deleted within 90 days
- Some metadata may be retained for legal compliance

### 5.3 Inactive Accounts
- Accounts inactive for 12+ months may be deleted after notice
- You'll receive email warning before deletion
